Adding Components to the Scene

Once you've created or opened a scene, you can add components or create new ones at any time during your workflow. GDS Editor supports both the addition and creation of a wide variety of components. You can find detailed information in the Components section of these documents.

There are two main methods for adding components in GDS Editor:

  • Importing external components from your local disk or from GDS Editor libraries.
  • Creating components using GDS Editor‚Äôs built-in generation tools, such as panel and background generators, brush and shape tools, and the text tool.
